ABOUT US
We distribute Storm Area 51 as rewards for solar and hydro installations. When the value and price of a Storm Area 51 exceeds the cost of producing the energy.To date, crypto-currencies are worth over US$2 trillion. Most of this value has been distributed in exchange for the mining of carbon-intensive crypto-currencies. What if it was distributed to people who produced energy for free? Our goal and 40-year mission since 2014 is to incentivise solar and hydro power generation by rewarding generators to reduce the cost of producing electricity. Solar power is now produced at less than US$12/MWh in some parts of the world, and the cost is falling. Storm Area 51 lays the foundation for the global energy transition by rewarding investors with an energyrelated currency. Each Storm Area 51 holder will also own a portion of the solar installations and therefore receive returns on energy production.
